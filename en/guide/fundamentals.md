# 🧭 Core Fundamentals

The Standard Feline Calendar (SFC) is a comprehensive system blending horology, astronomy, and feline ethology.

## ⏳ Time Dilation
Feline life flows differently. SFC uses a fixed scaling constant:
* **1 Earth Year = 5 Feline Years**
* **1 Earth Day = 1/73 Feline Year**

## 🍂 Feline Seasons
Feline seasons are driven by light and temperature:

* **Sprouting (Days 1-19):** Warming weather. Shedding peaks (99%). High restlessness.
* **Melting (Days 20-37):** Peak heat. Cats lose solid form and become "liquid," seeking cool spots.
* **Hoarding (Days 38-55):** Cooling down. Appetite surge to store fat for winter.
* **Sealing (Days 56-73):** Cold weather. Cats are "sealed" in blankets or by heaters. Minimal shedding (10%).
